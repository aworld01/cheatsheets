VIM 8.1.2269 (text editor)

    Normal mode.

  1. vi: to open VIM editor.
  2. :q: to exit from VIM.
  3. i: to goto insert mode. (for typeing.)
  4. press ESC: to goto normal mode.
  5. :w myFile.txt: to save file with name or save as.
  6. :w: to save file.
  7. :wq: to save and exit.
  8. :q!: to exit without save.
  9. :! ls: to fire Terminal command in VIM.
  10. :o myFile.txt: to open myFile.txt.
  11. vi 'filename': to open any file in VIM.
  12. v: to goto visual mode (for selection).
  13. V: to select full line.
  14. yy: to copy line.
  15. y: to copy selected text.
  16. u: to undo.
  17. CTRL + r: to redo.
  18. p: to paste line down.
  19. :put: paste with new line.
  20. cc: to cut the line and goto insert mode.
  21. dd: to cut line.
  22. d: to cut selected text.
  23. 5dd: to cut five line.
  24. gg: to goto start.
  25. SHIFT + g: to goto last line.
  26. 5gg: to goto line 5.
  27. :12: to goto line 12.
  28. 3 SHIFT g: to goto 3 line up.
  29. /fifth line: to find "fifth line" word in your document. n: to find next. N: to find next in reverse mode.
  30. ?fifth line: to find "fifth line" word from bottom. n: to find next.
  31. :%s/fourth/seventh: to replace all "fourth" word into "seventh" word.
  32. :%s/fourth/seventh/c: to replace "fourth" word by asking commands. (y/n/a/q/l/^E/^y)?
  33. :split file2.txt: to open two files horizontally at once.
  34. :vsplit file2.txt: to open two files vertically at once.
  35. CTRL w 'then auro'to navigate between two files.
  36. $: to goto end of the line.
  37. ^: to goto start of the line.
  38. ls /usr/share/vim/vim81/colors/: to see the list VIM themes.
  39. ls /usr/share/vim/vim81/colors/ > themes.txt: to print the list of VIM themes.
  40. colo desert: to apply the 'desert' named theme of VIM.
