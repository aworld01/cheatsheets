A visit to the Red Fort
Historical Buildings tell 'history of the past'. Delhi is famous for historical buildings. Jantar-Mantar, Jama Masjid, The Red Fort and Birla Mandir are worth seeing in Delhi. Last Sunday I went see the Red Fort. I hired a scooter and reached there. Ticket for entrance was bought. Then I went through Lahore Gate of Nakkarkhana. Two halls called Diwan-E-Khas arracted me inside the Red Fort. A few Gardens, Golden Mosque and Zanana were seen inside it. Prime Minister of India delivers message to the Nation on Independence day from the Red Fort. The Red Fort was built by Mughal Emperor named Shahjahan. It is made of red stones Its architecture is unlimitable. A museum is arranged in one of its buildings. Old arms, old pictures and old royal robes depict past history of India.
