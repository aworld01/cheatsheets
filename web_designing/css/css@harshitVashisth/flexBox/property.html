flexBox (Harshit Vashisth)

There are two direction(Axis) of flexBox:-
    1: Cross Axis (Left to Right)
    2: Main Axis (Top to Bottom)

There are two terms of flexBox:-
    1: flex-container
        a: display: flex;

        b: flex-direction: row;(default)
           flex-direction: row-reverse;
           flex-direction: column;
           flex-direction: column-reverse;

        c: flex-wrap: nowrap; (default)
           flex-wrap: wrap; (box{width: 400px;})
           flex-wrap: wrap-reverse;

        d: justify-content: flex-start; (defalut)
           justify-content: flex-end;
           justify-content: center;
           justify-content: space-between;
           justify-content: space-around;
           justify-content: space-evenly;

        e: align-items: stretch; (defalut) height: 200px;
           align-items: flex-start;
           align-items: flex-end;
           align-items: center;
        
        f: align-content: flex-start; flex-wrap: wrap;
           align-content: flex-end;
           align-content: center;
           align-content: space-between;
           align-content: space-around;

        This is the shorthand of "flex-direction" and "flex-wrap".
        g: flex-flow: column wrap;

    2: flex-items
        a: align-self: flex-start;
           align-self: flex-end;
           align-self: stretch; it's overwrites the "align-items".

        b: order: 0; (defalut)
           order: 1;
        
        c: flex-basis: 500px;

        d: flex-grow: 0; (defalut)
           flex-grow: 1;

         e: flex-shrink: 1;

                  flex-grow flex-shrink flex-basis (shorthand)
         f: flex: 1 2 400px;
