Right click on Desktop.
Goto:- New>Shortcut(shutdown -s -t 00)>Next(Shortcut name)>Finish

Right click on Shortcut that you made just.
Click on "Change Icon>OK"
Select the "Icon>OK>OK"

Note: shutdown -a (to abort shutdown command)

Shutdown: shutdown -s -t 00
Restart: shutdown -r -t 00
Log off: shutdown -l
