Windows 10 tricks

Make folder named: CON, NUL, PRN, AUX

Press: ALT + 255 / ALT + 0160 (by numeric key pad) then folder name.

Write your name at 'right click' menu:

  1. Press: Window key + R and type regedit then hit ENTER
  2. Goto: HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT/DesktopBackground(right click on Shell)/New/key/(Enter your name) hit ENTER
  3. Goto: Desktop (right click) your name will be there.

Disable USB ports:

  1. Press: Window key + R and type regedit then hit ENTER
  2. Goto: HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE/SYSTEM/CurrentControlSet/Services/USBSTOR/start(right click)/modify (change value "5") and click on OK
  3. Close everything and Restart you PC.

5 best refresh settings for Windows 10:

  1. Goto Task Manager and Disable unwanted programs.
  2. Press WIN + R and type: %temp% hit ENTER and delete all files there.
  3. Press WIN + R and type: temp hit ENTER and delete all files there.
  4. Press WIN + R and type: prefetch hit ENTER and delete all files there.
  5. Right click on C: drive, click on Properties/Disk Cleanup and clean the disk.
  6. Right click on C: drive, click on Properties//Tools/Check/Scane drive: to check the drive for file system errors.
  7. Right click on C: drive, click on Properties//Tools/Optimize
  8. Select drive and click on Optimize

Start Computer in safe mode:

  1. Press WIN + R and type: msconfig hit ENTER
  2. click on Boot/Safe boot/OK/Restart: then your computer will restart in safe mode.
