Removing Green-screen Background in Blender 3.0

to use node, click on + icon.
Goto:- General/Compositing[Use Nodes]

Shift+A, S: to search and add node
V: to zoom-out
Alt+V: to zoom-in

Require nodes:

  • Movie Clip: to import file.
  • Viewer: (Ctrl+Shift, Click on Movie Clip): to preview.
  • Keying: to remove background.
  • Composite: to export file.
