Blender

Spacebar: Play/Pause

TIMELINE

k: Cut
x: Delete
Ctrl+Z: Undo.
Home: Fit video on timeline.
Ctrl+End: Increase imported video time.
Ctrl+Scroll down: Move timeline from right to left.
Ctrl+Scroll up: Move timeline from left to right.
Shift+Scroll down: Move timeline from down to up.
Shift+Scroll up: timeline from up to down.
select videos+hold Left Click and move anywhere: Move freely.

PLAYHEAD

pg up: Move cursor at the end of timeline.
pg dn: Move cursor at the first of timeline.
Alt+pg up: Move right and goto center at video
Alt+pg down: Move left and goto center at video.
Shift+Left Arrow: Move timebar at first export video time.
Shift+Right Arrow: Move timebar at last export video time.
Select videos, Shift+S: Set video with first export video time.

Video Output settings

Resolution X: 1280p Y: 720p Frame Rate: 30fps File Format: FFmpeg Video Container: MPEG-4 Video Codec: H.264 Audio Codec: AAC Bitrate: 128
