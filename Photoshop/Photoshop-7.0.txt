Open photo:
    01. dubleClick at empty area to open photo.
    02. Goto: File/Open: to open photo.
    03. Ctrl+O: to open photo.

Ctrl+D: Deselect
EuroKey: to move Marquee Tool Selection.
Ctrl+Backspace: to fill background color.
Alt+Backspace: to fill foreground color.
Ctrl+Z: Undo
Ctrl+Alt+Z: Undo step by step
Ctrl+S: Save file.

Alt+scrollout: Zoom out
Alt+scrollIn: Zoom In

Ctrl+T: Free Transform
